Types of musical instruments

There are broadly three types of instruments. Below table sumarises each instrument types and best age to start learning

Instrument Type Description Examples Recommended Age
Percussion A percussion instrument produce sound by being struck or scraped by a beater. Drums, tabla, cymbals 5 years
String String instruments produce sound from vibrating strings when a performer plays the strings in some manner. Guitar, violin,ukulele 7 years
Digital An electronic instrument is any musical instrument that produces or modifies sounds by electric, and usually electronic, means. Digital pianos, keyboard, midi instruments 9 years
Best age to start learning any musical instrument 10 years
